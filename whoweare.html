Who We Are

At PETROENERGY INTL, we carefully listen to our clients, understand their project constrains, budget , Time scale safety and quality and. PEI act to provide the best leadership and organization to do the engineering work and produce design, deliverables and work plans to achieve the client requirements in terms of project completion on time, budget, constructability, safety, and sustainability.

PETRO ENERGY INTL LTD is now a British company aiming to provide a bridge of engineering excellence services between the Mideast and UK by newly adopted way of executing and providing the required engineering services by working remotely in line with COVID-19 health and softy requirement . The client will have the options to select and interview his own team for project management on line, to run and manage his project.

We provide a carefully selected specialized engineers and supplementary staff to lever complex and large scale plants keeping quality and schedule in-tact for procurement, construction and commissioning activities till the safe hand-over of the facility to our client’s operations teams.
