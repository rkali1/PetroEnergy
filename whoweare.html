Who We Are

Who is Petro Energy Intl. PEI

Petro Energy intl (PEI) was established in 2015 with a registration office in Dubai as a front end Marketing and Consulting Engineering service company set up to provide Marketing, project engineering management, Procurement,resourcing, Construction Management, Commissioning & Start-up services for the sectors of, Oil & Gas, Hydrocarbon Processing, petrochemical, Energy & Power, water treatment, Iron, Steel & Cement, Mining & Metallurgy, Transport, Health & Industrial training. PEI strive to provide its services with close continuous improvement for, safety and environment with quality on time budgeted results.

Petro Energy intl LTD is now a British company aiming to provide a bridge of engineering excellence services between the Mideast and UK by newly adopted way of executing and providing the required engineering services by working remotely in line with COVID-19 health and softy requirement . The client will have the options to select and interview his own team for project management on line, to run and manage his project.

PEI benefit from having both depth of the Mideast knowledge and link to al key service providers players and wide range of uncompromising management with sold technical experience gained through successful accomplishment of major projects .

PetroEnergy's strength is derived from it’s team’s cumulative depth of know-how of around 60 Years having worked with government & agencies in Pakistan, United Kingdom, France, USA and OPEC nations for projects in the Oil & Gas, Energy & Power, Mining & Metallurgy, Infrastructure, Allied-industry, Transport & Education.
